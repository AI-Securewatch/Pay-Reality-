# PAYREALITY MASTER PRODUCT DESIGN PROMPT

## Product Vision

Design the complete product experience for **PayReality**, the world's first **AI Authority Layer**.

PayReality enables organizations to safely delegate authority to AI systems while maintaining governance, accountability, compliance, auditability, and insurability.

The platform acts as an authority gateway between AI agents and real-world actions.

Every AI action is:

* Evaluated
* Authorized
* Governed
* Audited
* Logged
* Proven

through PayReality's proprietary architecture.

PayReality is not:

* An AI chatbot
* A cybersecurity dashboard
* A compliance checklist
* A fintech payment app

PayReality is:

**The System of Record for AI Authority**

The platform becomes the trusted source of truth whenever an organization asks:

* What did the AI do?
* Why did it do it?
* Who authorized it?
* Was policy followed?
* Can we prove it?
* Can we insure it?

# Design Principles

### Principle 1

Authority before intelligence.

The product is not about what AI can do.

It is about controlling what AI is allowed to do.

---

### Principle 2

Evidence over claims.

Every important action should visually show evidence.

---

### Principle 3

Trust through visibility.

Every decision path should be explainable.

---

### Principle 4

Enterprise-grade simplicity.

Powerful beneath the surface.

Simple above the surface.
